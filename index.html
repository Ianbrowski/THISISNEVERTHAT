About Me

Hi, I’m John Christian A. Tejada. I’m someone who enjoys both creating and observing—quietly taking in the world while also building things that reflect how I see it.
I’ve always been drawn to art. There’s something special about being able to freeze a moment, an emotion, or an idea and turn it into something lasting. Art helps me express what’s hard to say—it gives meaning to the little things and lets me hold on to what matters.
I also love coding. There’s a certain satisfaction in making something from nothing, in solving problems and building systems that work. It’s creative in its own way—like constructing your own world, one line at a time.
Even with everything I focus on, I still appreciate the small, simple things—whether it’s a good snack, a quiet moment, or just taking time to enjoy life. Those little things help keep me grounded.
Right now, I’m aiming to build a future where I can do meaningful work while living freely and independently. Success, for me, means having the space to grow, create, and live life on my own terms.
Thanks for taking a moment to get to know me—a mix of creativity and logic, always learning, always building.
